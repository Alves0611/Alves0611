## 🚀 About Me

</div>

I’m a **Platform Engineer and SRE** focused on building reliable internal platforms, scalable cloud infrastructure, and operational systems that help engineering teams move faster with safety and consistency.

My work sits at the intersection of **platform engineering**, **cloud-native operations**, **observability**, **GitOps**, and **AI-driven workflows** — with a strong focus on creating foundations that are reproducible, secure, and production-ready.

I’m especially interested in:
- **Internal Developer Platforms (IDPs)** and self-service engineering experiences
- **Kubernetes platform operations** and progressive delivery patterns
- **Observability engineering**, incident readiness, and operational excellence
- **Infrastructure as Code** and repeatable cloud architecture
- **AIOps**, AI agents, and automation for platform/SRE workflows
